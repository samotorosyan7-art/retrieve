Tax Law & Compliance

Overview

 Navigating tax regulations in Armenia can be challenging. We specialize in tax law, ensuring compliance while optimizing financial outcomes. Whether you are a business or an individual, our team simplifies tax complexities and protects your interests.

How We Can Help

  • Comprehensive tax planning and strategy
  • Resolving tax disputes with Armenian authorities
  • Cross-border taxation solutions
  • Transfer pricing documentation and compliance
  • Advising on VAT, customs, and excise duties
  • Providing legal representation during tax audits and investigations
  • Offering guidance on double tax treaties and international agreements
  • Advising on tax-efficient investment structures for individuals and companies
  • Helping with tax registration and reporting for new businesses
  • Drafting and submitting appeals in tax-related disputes
  • Assisting in VAT refunds and customs clearance issues
  • Monitoring changes in Armenian tax regulations to ensure compliance

Why Choose Us?

  • In-depth understanding of Armenian tax laws and international treaties.
  • Proven track record in achieving favorable outcomes for clients.
  • Strategies designed to mitigate risks and maximize benefits.

Frequently Asked Questions

What legal support can you provide during a tax audit?

We assist clients by preparing required documentation, representing them in discussions with Armenian tax authorities, and ensuring their rights are protected throughout the audit process. If disputes arise, we advocate on your behalf to resolve them effectively.

How can legal counsel help with VAT and customs compliance?

Legal advice is crucial for navigating VAT and customs regulations, particularly in complex scenarios involving imports, exports, or supply chains. We review contracts, advise on tax obligations, and assist in securing refunds or resolving disputes with customs authorities.

Can you handle tax disputes in court?

Yes, we provide full representation in tax litigation cases. From filing appeals against tax assessments to presenting your case in Armenian courts, we offer strategic legal support to ensure the best possible outcome.

What role does legal advice play in cross-border taxation?

Cross-border transactions often involve intricate tax regulations and risks of double taxation. We provide legal advice to structure transactions, leverage double taxation treaties, and ensure compliance with both Armenian and international laws.

How can you help with tax-related contract drafting? 

We ensure that contracts, especially those involving financial transactions or international trade, are structured to comply with Armenian tax laws while minimizing potential liabilities. This includes drafting clauses that address VAT, withholding taxes, and other obligations.
